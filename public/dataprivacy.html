Data Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Bazario GmbH (hereinafter "Bazario", "we", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our online platform "Bazario" (hereinafter "Platform").

2. Data Controller

Bazario GmbH

Switzerland
Email: info@bazario.com

3. Data We Collect

3.1 Registration Data: When you create an account, we collect your first name, last name, username, email address, date of birth, and profile picture (optional).

3.2 Transaction Data: When you buy or sell items, we collect details such as product listings, purchase history, payment information (processed via third-party providers), and shipping addresses.

3.3 Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with the Platform, including IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.

3.4 Reviews and Ratings: Any reviews or ratings you submit are stored and linked to your account.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

4.1 To provide and operate the Platform, including account management, transaction processing, and user support.

4.2 To facilitate communication between Buyers and Sellers.

4.3 To improve our services through analytics and user feedback.

4.4 To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or misuse.

5. Legal Basis

5.1 Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary to fulfill our obligations under the General Terms and Conditions (GTC).

5.2 Consent: Certain data processing (e.g., optional profile pictures) is based on your consent.

5.3 Legitimate Interest: We process data to improve the Platform and ensure its security.

5.4 Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws (e.g., tax regulations).

6. Data Sharing

6.1 With Other Users: Your username, ratings, and reviews are visible to other users. Shipping addresses are shared with Sellers for delivery purposes.

6.2 Third Parties: We use payment processors (e.g., Mastercard, Visa) and shipping providers who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

6.3 Legal Requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

7. Data Retention

7.1 We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Transaction data is kept for 10 years to comply with Swiss tax laws.

7.2 Upon account deletion, personal data is anonymized or deleted, except where retention is legally required.

8. Your Rights

8.1 You have the right to access, rectify, or delete your personal data, restrict processing, or object to it. Contact us at info@bazario.com to exercise these rights.

8.2 You may withdraw consent at any time (e.g., by removing optional data from your profile).

8.3 You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (e.g., the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner).

9. Cookies and Tracking

9.1 We use cookies to enhance your experience (e.g., session management, analytics). You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings.

10. Data Security

10.1 We implement technical and organizational measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Changes to This Policy

11.1 We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on the Platform, and significant updates will be communicated via email.
